PLEASE READ THIS SECTION CAREFULLY.

mind² ECHO generates behavioral profiles based on the mind² method by Dr. Thumm GmbH. These profiles describe communication preferences observed in a single conversation. They are not:

  • A psychological diagnosis or assessment
  • A personality test or classification
  • A substitute for professional psychological evaluation
  • A basis for employment, promotion, or personnel decisions

3.2 Limitations

mind² profiles:

  • Reflect behavior in one specific conversation, not stable personality traits
  • May vary across different conversations and contexts

  • Are generated by AI and may contain inaccuracies
