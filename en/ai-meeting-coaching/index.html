AI meeting coaching, grounded in what was actually said.

AI meeting coaching turns a real conversation into concrete, personal feedback. After a meeting, mind² ECHO gives every speaker behaviour and content tips drawn from what they actually said: strengths to lean on, blind spots to watch, what to try next time. Each tip is backed by a quote from the transcript, so the advice is specific, not generic.

What AI meeting coaching does

Most feedback after a meeting is vague, or never happens at all. AI meeting coaching reads the transcript and gives each person a short, concrete set of suggestions tied to their own words. Not a grade and not a ranking: a few things that went well and a few things to try, each anchored to a moment you can actually point to.

Specific, because it has the evidence

Generic advice like "be more concise" or "listen more" is easy to ignore. mind² ECHO quotes the exact moment a point got lost in a long aside, or where a good question opened the room up, so the tip lands. Behaviour tips are about how something came across; content tips are about what was raised, missed, or left unresolved.

It coaches, it does not judge

mind² ECHO never compares speakers against each other and never reduces anyone to a type. Each person’s coaching stands on its own, framed as how they communicated in this meeting and what might help next time. It is a mirror for reflection, not a scorecard, and people communicate differently from one room to the next.

Built on a per-speaker profile

The coaching builds on the same mind² profile mind² ECHO draws for each speaker: the behaviour dimensions they leaned on in this meeting. Knowing someone came across very direct, or very detail-driven, is what lets the suggestions fit the person, instead of being one-size-fits-all.
